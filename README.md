## About me

I'm a Full Stack Developer with **4+ years of experience**, passionate about building interfaces that make sense for the people using them. My work lives at the intersection of code and product — I write business logic just as much as I think about visual hierarchy, and I rarely manage to separate the two.

I've developed a broad understanding of the product cycle, which allows me to make technical decisions grounded in real context, not just specifications. I also integrate AI tools like **Claude AI** and **Cursor** into my workflow to amplify delivery with more quality and speed.
